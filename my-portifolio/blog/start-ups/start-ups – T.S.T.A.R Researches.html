Starting A Business: A Universal Guide.

April 30, 2020April 30, 2020 Tinotenda JoeLeave a comment

Do you want to start a business? If YES, here is a universal guide to starting your business with NO money and no experience

Before going into any business, it is highly recommended that you do proper feasibility studies to know exactly what it will take to run a business legally and ethically.

Universally, business registration is a prerequisite and going through all the regular procedures of setting up a legal business entity.

Here is a short list of the aspects you will need to consider.

  • An overview of the industry
  • Market research and feasibility studies
  • Distinguish niches within the business that you can specialize in
  • Analyzing the level of competition
  • Legal matters

An overview of the industry.

When seeking to enter any type of business, it is important to pay close attention to the information at hand, that is studying the growth rate, revenues and profits. This will prove useful in finding out market opportunities to leverage. Giving an undivided attention to the specific area of concentration can make the business even more profitable upon establishment and stress free for the owner

Market research and feasibility studies

By definition this is the process of systematically gathering, recording and analyzing data and information about customers, competitors and the market. Its uses include to help create a business plan, launch a new product or service, fine tune existing products and services, and expand into new markets. With an effective market research strategy, you will be able to determine which portion of the population will purchase a product or service, based on variables like age, gender, location and income level. Research can be broken down into the following parts:

  • Market trends
  • Market segmentation
  • Market information
  • Market research for business planning

These studies provide you with important information that will drive your business away from entering blindly into risky businesses.

Distinguish niches within the business that you can specialize in.

Giving an in depth analysis of the different niche in the field you want to establish your business is crucial. In the end you must be able to pick out a single niche. This is because if you try to cater to everyone your overall marketing and message won’t speak to anyone. Your specific niche can also be linked to who your ideal client is. So, it’s important you have a targeted specific market and speak to a customer or client in a certain way which they can really relate to.

The level of competition.

As obvious as it is, there is hardly any business venture that does not have its fare share of competition. There will always be quite a number of players already making impact in the, same field you want to start your business. With proper information, networking and hard work, anyone can still penetrate the wall created by the competition and develop a space for himself.

You will need to study the greats, analyze those known brands in the game. Lets is be known that big brands are not just competitors, there are examples to follow. Most times, it pays to study what these brands are up to, then focus on the part of the market they may be neglecting. One other positive thing observing these brands can do for one’s business is to give one a model to follow.

Another hidden thing most people do not always see is that being a bench player and benchmarking is one of the major strategies that can make a business stand out. This is just the process of observing another business with similar value chain activities with the aim of looking for ways to implement key practices in one’s own company.

Legal matters.

Due to the possible exposure to conditions that can warrant lawsuits either from customers, partners and or competitors, It is therefore important that before you start running your business you find yourself a lawyer who will process all your legal concerns. These include insurance policies, securing your trademark and intellectual property protection rights. Following proper legal documentation for a business will prevent you from crushing into unforeseeable lawsuits.

In conclusion, there will always be lot of aspects to put into consideration, but when you’re through with the crucial aspects you will be needing to come up with a marketing plan, assessing the possible ways to boost your customer retention and your brand awareness in so doing creating your corporate identity.

How to develop your offline personal branding.

July 15, 2019July 15, 2019 Tinotenda Joe1 Comment

Offline Personal branding strategies follow the same basic principles of Online but the applications are now in the physical world. Aspects like defining a clear target audience, narrowing down with your specific audience, standing for something, having a unique voice,
giving value first, making it about your customers, etc., all will remain the same.

With that being said, building your brand offline also have the added advantages of physical presence and individual touch to your personal brand. The following added options, when used correctly together with the online approach , can be extremely powerful.

-Networking with influencers at public gatherings and conferences

Grow your network by connecting with other members in your field. Join groups and participate in activities that relates to your field. Make your self visible to the extent that your name becomes synonymous within your field . Reach out to influencers and show them your worth by providing value upfront to impress them, and thus building a connection with these high-quality individuals in your field.

–Conducting public speaking.

Your appearances has to be both online and offline. The best way to have an impactful appearance offline is by doing public speaking. Give out power or helpful presentations. Make the audience see with their own eyes what you have been talking online. This way you will strengthen your transparency and also you will be able to make new customers out of strangers.

Though these and others are important aspects to consider when developing a personal brand, i am sure you do not need your brand to be perceived as a weak brand. To strengthen your personal brand you need to be consistent and always recognize your main source of strength. Successful brands in general possess measurable features that distinguish them from other brands. Notice that what you are very good at put much emphasis on it.

Keep on adding value to your utility promises. Try by all means to provide clear answers to what it is you will be claiming. Make it easier for a customer to picture your services in your absence, make it easier for them to navigate through your services.

If you intend to be a good sales person provide them with clear reasons why and how you can be better than the other sales people. That way you will be able to stand out.

Goodluck!

Four Fundamentals Of Personal Branding

July 9, 2019July 12, 2019 Tinotenda Joe2 Comments

Following this brief introduction on my personal branding episodes, let us step into the fundamentals needed to build a personal brand. These fundamentals will separate you from many people who only perceive personal branding as a tool for businesses. Your Branding follows you every where you go. As for me i consider these fundamentals as blueprints of a floor plan, you will not build anything in their absence.

Your personal brand is everything that encompasses who you are, who you associate with, your beliefs and what you stand for.

Fundamentals :

1 Define You Values – before you start building your personal brand, list down all the qualities that explain your values. Make another list of values of the people you interact with frequently. Draft a third list of the values of your employer, employees, your competitors, your advisors or clients.

Now combine these 3 lists by extracting the values that have one or two things in common from each list and make a new list of values from this comparison. The result is what you can use to pinpoint what are your actual values.

2.Personify Your Values – Now imagine if your values were something tangible like a cellphone or a car, what type of a cellphone or a car would they be? List down all the attributes of each in terms of style, tone, messaging etc.

3 Determine (picture) The End Result Of Your Values – write down where and who you want to be in five years from now. Do not limit your imagination, picture beyond what you think is impossible.

4 Asses Where You Are Currently – assess where your are right now and identify what you will need to do to achieve the imagination you have done at stage 3. Review all the aspects that are associated with you or your business brand, your personal style, the way you carry yourself, who you associate with, your resume and your social channels.

5 Begin Branding Yourself – by following fundamental one upto four you will be in possession of the blueprints of your True Brand. Keep these fundamentals in check everytime and also use them to decide if the conted you post fit who you are and want to become.

A successful brand takes time to develop. like completing a puzzle focus on collecting small pieces at a time,eventually you will make a significant personal brand.

Personal Branding

July 8, 2019July 12, 2019 Tinotenda Joe2 Comments

Abstract :

Personal Branding is the key to
success of your business or your career in this world of stiff competition. This is because it has the power to shift you from the rat race where everyone and businesses are competing to get out into the fast track of Branding, where there are endless opportunities. Recently people start making their buying and hiring decisions based on the brand of a person behind a
product or service. People now want to see if the person backing the product has a reliable personal brand which has authority in the industry. So it does not really matter whou you are, wheter you are an entrepreneur, CEO or an employee, you will need to build a personal brand. You will need to make it great if your plans are to get ahead in life.

By definition Personal Branding is a marketing strategy that articulates your skills to stand out from other professionals in your industry.Your personal brand helps to express your unique value to a customer, an employer, and differentiates your skills, experiences, and abilities from your competition.

The essence here is standing out. When you stand out in whatever it is you do, people recognize you. They see you as a thought leader. This enables you to create a position from where you can get more audience
and charge higher prices. Also, your network will become richer.

As with a successful brand, your personal brand should be unique, credible, consistent, and relevant. Personal branding is not only important for your business, it is also an important part of the internship or job search when it comes to the job market. It also improves your likelihood of finding a good fit within a company and it gives you increased self-confidence and focus in whatever it is you do.

Importance of personal branding:

1. Recognition

Since it raises awareness of your services, it gives you recognition from your target audience and will also open a lot of closed doors and opportunities. This, in turn,
gives you a headstart over the competition.

2. Networking opportunities

When you are Branding your self the right way, you will easily attract the right influencers and business leaders of top companies, this will give you a quick jumpstart in your career or business because you will be in a position to form relationships with these influential people and exponentially grow your business
in the right direction.

3. Creates opportunities to make more money

When you build your Personal Brand, your voice becomes synonymous to the voice of your industry. You become the symbol of influence, trust, authority, authenticity, and quality in your industry. This can lead to high
paying work opportunities and higher prices for your product.

4. Higher productivity

Branding comes with responsibility, the more you become responsible, the more you will be productive. When you see the results of your hard work with people starting to notice you, acknowledge you, and refer to you, you feel a sense of satisfaction, pride and power. These results make you gravitate more and more to your work and hence make you
more productive.

Recognize the importance of personal branding in your business, career or life in general, and start building…

Brand Strategy (how to build it?)

June 30, 2019July 15, 2019 Tinotenda Joe1 Comment

Brand strategy can be defined in many ways, it can be defined as a long-term plan for the development of a successful brand in order to achieve specific goals. Every business needs a brand, a strong brand and I’ve mentioned the reasons why here branding what is it? why is it vital?. Branding is good, but you need to possess a strategy in the way you brand.

The first essential aspect when formulating your brand strategy is to be able to answer these questions

>why is this brand important to your business?

>what do you want to achieve from your branding?

If you understand much about goal setting you will see that this part is more like outlining your goals. Make sure you parallel your business goals with your branding strategy. When you do this, think in long terms not short terms so that you will be able to deduce long term brand strategy.

Secondly, you will need to understand the nature or structure of your business’s market, do a proper market research. Get to know the weaknesses and strengths of your competitors. Also get to know your customers. This will enables you to come up with an excellent market approach and the nature or the architect of the product you will bring to the market.

These two activities will make sure that you understand your brand. You will get to know what differentiates your brand from others. Now you will need to engage with a designer, be it your advisors or a group of people outside your business who will help you creatively translate your strategy ideas into a brand identity system which you can as key influence of your business.

Creating a brand strategy requires so many things to think about, for instance the ways you are going to allocate your business’s resources knowing that every dollar you spend on your brand means one less dollar you could have spend on anything else. This is important because it gives you a chance to allocate enough budget to do your brand justice. Don’t spread your brand and marketing dollars too thin and also don’t spread too much of your dollars. Just balance your equation because there is no fixed amount of money you will have to spend in this.

In Susan Gunelias words

(CEO of KeySplash Creative Inc., a marketing communications and strategic branding company.)

Your brand strategy must ne flexible as the makert, competitors and customers change.

She also illustrated the best ways you can think of this flexibility with these 3 statements.

  1. Just as your goals in life might change over time, so might your brand goals.
  2. Similarly, just as you might modify your plan to achieve your goals in life, so too might your brand marketing plan change.
  3. Finally, just as you seize opportunities to move closer to your goals as they arise throughout your life, you’ll also seize short-term opportunities to grow your brand and move closer to achieving your long-term brand goals as those opportunities are presented to you.

    In conclusion, when creating a strategy, take your time. Do not rush. When you give in your time you will give your self a chance to avoid the formulation of short term strategies. Just like success, brands are not built overnight, be a brand architect, design a lasting structure to bridge brand strategy and brand messaging. Without a strong brand foundation built on a well-defined strategy, brands have little chance for success

How To Successfully Launch A Startup: (from idea to a thriving company)

June 17, 2019June 17, 2019 Tinotenda Joe2 Comments

We both know that big companies like Facebook, Microsoft, Amazon and other giant companies having the lead in the business field today haven’t started as big as they are now. In the beginning they were all ideas followed by proper execution which led them to where they are today.

As the world grows bigger interms of population and data so are the problems. This is giving rise to people coming up with different ways of solving those problems. One of the ways is through innovative startups, these startups are aiming at providing solutions to the people so that in return they expand their sizes and keep on providing more and more valuable and profitable services.

Many startup businesses today are struggling to Scale-Up because of some technical problems and some people (many of them who have the passion) are stuck with ideas, failing to execute those ideas. Here i will discuss some of the ways people can use to launch their start-up businesses step by step from an idea. The steps are as follows :

I. Thinking outside of the box

II. Being able to identify the reasons why you want to start a business

III. Identifying the main problem you will be solving

IIV. Double checking if the problem is really a problem

V. The Need to provide services that actually solve the problems practically

VI. The market research needed

VII. Assembling the appropriate data

VIII. Assembling your team

I) Thinking Outside Of The Box

Establishing a business or even running a business requires you to be able to think out side of the box. To think big. You do not need to have a limited thinking capacity or to limit your ability to bring your creativity into action because of some customs.

An innovative mindset is required for you to be successful in establishing your company. This is because in the long run you will be facing many challenges, more than you can think of right now and these challenges will require you to make quick informed decisions that will benefit your business at large.

The first step for you to teach your self to think big is to begin with a picture of how you want your company to be like 10 years from now? Be prepared to brainstorm, to be creative all of the times.

Allow flexibility in your thinking, avoid using the thinking boundaries at all costs. If you are having trouble with the think inside or outside the box mantra, lose that box, it is even much better to have no starting or finishing points.

So when it comes to building your startup,

do not think inside your startup’s box, always think outside of the box or pretend like you don’t even know where the box is!

II) You Must Be Able To Identify The Reasons Why You Want To Start A Business.

In business, you will not create a product or provide services without a competent cause. There is no such thing. You have to be sure why you have decided to venture into that specific niche of your startup business .

Attain a level where you have answers for questions that begin with”why?”. The answers doesn’t matter, maybe it is because you want to help people, maybe you want to be rich or maybe you want to help people and get money in so doing or maybe you are just crazy about creating things. Just have the answers. This will help you to create a clear picture on your mind, a clear path. That said you will realize decision making will be a lot easier.

III. Identify the main problem you will be solving

Assuming that you have the ability to thing big, you have the reasons why you want to establish a business, then i guess you will already have a problem you are keen to solve and bringing solutions to the society.

If not you will need to study your play ground. Notice if there are any gaps in the society that you want to fill in.

IV. Double checking if the problem is really a problem

Not all problems are problems and whilst there can be acute and chronic problems, it is important that you classify the problems you’ve identified. See what happens if you remove that problem from the equation. If nothing changes then that problem is not worth much of your time and effort. See also if there is anything apart from the individuals that is being affected by the gap you have identified, or the problem you want to solve. See if there are other established systems that at one point are being pulled back with those gaps.

This can be a difficult part to distinguish what problems really matters and gaps that are worth your services. Endemic or ecdemic, you will have to double check the problem you want to solve because maybe someone is already solving that problem in a different way than yours. This brings in the essence of competition in business, will talk about this later

V. checking if the services you will be offering really solve the problem practically

Many startups skip this stage because they think they have all the tools to start building and providing their services. In the long run they will fallback, no sales. This is because their solutions were solving the problems theoretically and filling in the gaps so they didn’t consider the practical matters

It is important that before you start pitching your idea and building your empire you consider the practical fundamentals of your solution. Check if the solution is cost effective? Do three or more surveys to see the reaction of your customers towards the proposed solution and also listen to what they say. This way you will realize what they want not what you want to give them. Round up their framework of needs with your solution and withdraw a final approach,this will be the best.

VI. Market research Is Needed

You can come up with a brilliant idea, and you can execute that idea the smart way but without the appropriate market research you will sell your services to nobody this is because a good market research will help you to come up with a product or service that will perfectly fit into the makert, if it is a product, market research will help you to design a compelling architect that customers need.

When doing a market research try to consider these questions :

Who are you selling to?

Where are they going to buy it?

How much would they pay for your services?

Who are your competitors? What are the competing products?

What is the cost to deploy your products or services?

assess these questions before you create anything with your startup. The more you get the best answers the more you will provide good service, the more chances yoi will have for your startup to be successful.

VII. Assemble the appropriate data

This step assumes that you have achieved the best out of the previous steps because you will not be able to collect necessary data if you do not know the specifications of your startup i.e what it does? Specifications of the preferred market.

Now all you will need is to collect specific data not grabbing everything about growth that comes your way because you now have a vision of your startup and you what is needed, what information you have to feed your startup, the fundamental approaches you need to get your business off the ground. Collect more data as much as you can because more data means you will have well informed decisions which means better products or results, which means more interested customers.

VIII. Assemble your best team

you have all( the prerequisites) what it takes to execute your idea. To bring life to your startup. All you have left with is a team, you can’t go at it alone. launching business requires a team that is as dedicated as you are or even more than you’re.

Select a group of at least 3 people at first, more if possible. As your startup expand add more people. it can be difficult to find people with the same vision as yours and willing to work on something that pays little to nothing but you will find it a lot more easier if you keep on adding a single person each time.

In steve Jobs words:

“when you get a core group of ten great people, it becomes self policing as to who the other members let into that group”

So just gather a mastermind in the beginning, that mastermind will grow itself.

The most important thing is to just get started, stop hesitating, stop waiting for the right time, just get started with what you have right now.

Do you have that idea? Do you think its epic? Then what are you still waiting for?
